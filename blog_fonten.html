Songwriters Font - fonts based on the handwriting of musicians

21.04.2018
ru/en


Hi,
On April 10, many magazines published an article about the Songwriters font, which is based on the handwriting of famous musicians.
They used the manuscripts of Bowie, Lennon, Cobain, Gainsbourg, Cohen and everything would be okay, but after ~3 days website
rightholders filed a complaint and the site was closed:

Later, some publications published an article about the closure of the site and now it is more difficult to find these fonts, but it is still possible

Download: David Bowie, John Lennon, Kurt Cobain, Leonard Cohen, Serge Gainsbourg.

Font created by French graphic designer Nicolas Damiens and copywriter Julien Sans
